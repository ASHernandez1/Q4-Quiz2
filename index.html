Characters in Noli Me Tanghere
Pilosopo Tasio Pilosopo Tasio is an old scholar who lives in San Diego. Although he is smart some think he's out of his mind because of his crazy ideas and his perceptions on Christianity.
Pedro Pedro is the husband of Sisa. He's also the father of Basilio and Crispin. He is a representation of how abusive the relationships of families were back then.

P h o t o G a l l e r y

The Philippines is an archipelago located in Southeast Asia known for its unique culture, flavorful cuisines, friendly citizens, and most especially its breath-taking tourist spots. Having so many wonderful places ranging from vibrant coral reefs all the way to majestic mountains and volcanoes, it's quite hard to pick a location for your perfect vacation; with this, here are 3 recommended areas for you to visit and explore.


Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are a huge agricultural area located in the Northern parts of the Philippines. These were sculpted into the mountains by the Ifugao tribe of the Philippines. Even after 2000 years, these terraces still remain in use by the local people for agriculture and their daily living.

Mount Pulag

One of the tallest mountains in the Philippines and the Luzon area. Hikers who trek to its summit are rewarded with an amazing view of the "sea of clouds" surrounding it. Being one of the tallest points in the country, you can even spot the peaks of its surrounding provinces.

Mount Pinatubo

Mount Pinatubo is a volcano located in Luzon. After its eruption in 1991, it left behind a massive crater lake at the top. Today, hikers and adventure seekers visit its peak to swim and boat on the lake's pristine waters.

About The Band

Arctic Monkeys is an indie rock band formed in Sheffield, South Yorkshire, England in 2002 after meeting at Stocksbridge High School. The band consists of Alex Turner (vocals, guitar, piano), Jamie Cook (guitar), Nick O'Malley (backing vocals, bass) and Matt Helders (drums, vocals).


Fun Fact! All the Band Members have their own side projects aside from Arctic Monkeys. One of the side projects being, Death Ramps, another band which consists of all the Arctic Monkeys members along with 2 additional members.

Alexander David Turner (born 6 January 1986) is an English musician, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is well known as the front man and principal songwriter of the rock band Arctic Monkeys, with whom he has released seven albums. He has also recorded with his side project involving Miles Kane, as The Last Shadow Puppets and also as a solo artist.

Matthew Helders (born 7 May 1986) is an English drummer, vocalist and songwriter. He is best known as a founding member of Arctic Monkeys, with whom he has recorded five studio albums.

Jamie Robert Cook (born 8 July 1985) is an English guitarist and songwriter. He is best known as the rhythm and lead guitarist and a founding member of the indie rock band Arctic Monkeys, with whom he has recorded six studio albums.

Nicholas O'Malley (born 5 July 1985) is the bass guitarist and backing vocalist of English band Arctic Monkeys.
